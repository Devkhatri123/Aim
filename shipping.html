Shipping & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. Digital Service Delivery

Aim is a digital application and service platform. Our services are delivered digitally through our website and mobile application. There is no physical shipping involved in our service delivery.

2. Service Activation

Timeline: Your subscription will be activated immediately upon successful payment processing.

  • Instant activation after payment confirmation
  • You will receive a confirmation email with login credentials within 5 minutes
  • Full access to all subscribed features will be available immediately

3. Free Trial Period

We offer a 7-day free trial for new users to experience our premium features at no cost. No payment is required to access the trial.

  • Trial period: 7 days from account creation
  • Full access to all premium features during trial
  • Auto-renewal reminder sent 24 hours before trial ends

4. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time without penalty or additional charges.

4.1 How to Cancel

  • Log into your account
  • Navigate to Settings > Subscription
  • Click on "Cancel Subscription"
  • Confirm your cancellation
  • You will receive a cancellation confirmation email

4.2 Cancellation Timeline

  • Immediate Cancellation: If cancelled before the auto-renewal date, your access will continue until the end of your current billing period
  • Pro-rated Refund: If cancelled within 24 hours of payment, you are eligible for a full refund
  • Mid-cycle Cancellation: Cancellations after 24 hours will not be refunded but will stop future charges

4.3 Cancellation Confirmation

A cancellation confirmation will be sent to your registered email address immediately. Your subscription status will be updated within 24 hours.

5. Trial Cancellation

You can cancel your free trial at any time before it expires. To cancel your trial:

  • Go to Settings > Subscription
  • Click "Cancel Trial"
  • Confirm cancellation
  • Your account will be downgraded to the free plan immediately

6. Cancellation Restrictions

Cancellation cannot be processed if:

  • Your account is suspended due to violations of our Terms of Service
  • There are pending disputes or refund claims
  • Your account is flagged for fraudulent activity

7. Reactivation

Timeline: You can reactivate your subscription at any time within 30 days of cancellation without losing your account data.

  • Reactivation is instantaneous upon payment
  • All your previous settings and preferences will be restored
  • After 30 days, your account data may be permanently deleted

8. International Access

Aim is accessible globally. Our digital service is available in:

  • All countries with internet access
  • No geographic restrictions for service delivery
  • Available on all devices with supported browsers and operating systems

9. Automatic Renewal

Your subscription will automatically renew on the scheduled date unless cancelled beforehand.

  • Renewal notifications sent 7 days before renewal
  • Final reminder sent 24 hours before renewal
  • Charges will appear on your original payment method
