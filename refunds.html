Refunds, Returns & Exchange Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. Overview

Aim is a digital subscription service. Since we provide digital services rather than physical products, our refund, returns, and exchange policies are tailored to digital service delivery.

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • 24-Hour Refund Window: Full refund if requested within 24 hours of purchase
  • Technical Issues: Refund if unable to access service due to technical problems on our end
  • Billing Errors: Refund if charged incorrectly or multiple times
  • Service Unavailability: Refund if service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  • Email: support@aimfacts.com with subject "Refund Request"
  • Include your account email and order ID
  • Provide detailed reason for the refund request
  • Attach any supporting documentation (screenshots, error messages, etc.)

4. Refund Processing Timeline

Processing Time: 5-7 business days from approval

  • Step 1 - Review: We will review your refund request within 2-3 business days
  • Step 2 - Approval/Rejection: We will notify you of approval or rejection within 3-5 business days
  • Step 3 - Processing: Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Step 4 - Bank Processing: Your bank may take an additional 3-5 business days to credit the amount

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following cases:

  • Refund requested after 24 hours of purchase (except for technical/billing issues)
  • Service cancelled by user after 24 hours without technical issues
  • Account suspended due to violation of Terms of Service
  • Fraudulent or dispute activity detected on account
  • Multiple refund requests for the same transaction
  • Service accessed and used for more than 24 hours

6. Plan Downgrades (Exchange)

Timeline: Downgrades are processed immediately with pro-rated adjustments.

You can downgrade from a higher tier subscription to a lower tier. The difference in pricing will be handled as follows:

  • If you've overpaid: Credit will be applied to your next billing cycle
  • If you owe additional fees: You will be informed before downgrade is processed
  • Downgrade takes effect immediately in your account
  • Next renewal will be at the new plan's pricing

7. Plan Upgrades (Exchange)

Timeline: Upgrades are processed immediately.

You can upgrade to a higher tier subscription at any time:

  • Difference in price will be charged immediately
  • You will receive a new invoice reflecting the upgrade
  • Upgrade takes effect immediately in your account
  • New renewal date will be set based on the upgrade

8. Trial Period Refund Policy

7-Day Free Trial: No charges, no refund needed

  • Free trial requires no payment method for activation
  • No refund is applicable as the trial is completely free
  • Simply cancel before trial ends to avoid charges

9. Partial Refunds

In cases where you request a refund after partial use:

  • Refunds within 24 hours of purchase: Full refund
  • Technical issues: Pro-rated refund based on service unavailability
  • Billing errors: Full amount of overcharge refunded

10. Refund Method

Refunds will be credited to your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund appears in 3-5 business days
  • Digital Wallet: Refund appears in 1-2 business days
  • Bank Transfer: Refund appears in 5-7 business days

11. Dispute Resolution

If you dispute a refund decision or have concerns:

  • Contact our support team within 30 days of the decision
  • Provide additional documentation or evidence
  • We will review and provide a final decision within 10 business days
  • See our Complaints & Support Policy for escalation procedures

12. Contact Information

For refund, return, or exchange inquiries:

  • Phone: +92-300-1234567
  • Support Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Pakistan Standard Time)

13. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your continued use of our service indicates acceptance of any modifications to this policy.
