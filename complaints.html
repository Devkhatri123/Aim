Complaints & Support Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. How to Contact Us

If you have any complaints or issues, please reach out to us using the following contact details:

  • Email: support@aimfacts.com
  • Phone: +92-300-1234567
  • Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Pakistan Standard Time)

2. Simple Steps to File a Complaint

  • Step 1: Send an email to support@aimfacts.com with your complaint details
  • Step 2: Include your account email and description of the issue
  • Step 3: We will acknowledge your complaint within 24-48 hours
  • Step 4: We will investigate and provide a solution within 7 business days

3. Resolution Timeline

  • Initial Response: 24-48 hours
  • Investigation & Solution: 7 business days

4. What to Include in Your Complaint

  • Your full name and email address
  • Account username (if applicable)
  • Description of the issue
  • Screenshots or any supporting details

5. We're Here to Help

We take all complaints seriously and are committed to resolving them fairly and promptly. Your feedback helps us improve our services. Please don't hesitate to reach out!
